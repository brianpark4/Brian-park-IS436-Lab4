Undergraduate Programs

UMBC is ranked top 10 most innovative school in the US. The US Information Systems department is proud of our traditional undergraduate program and continues to do so. We offer a variety of majors, minors, and certificates to help reach your potential.

Business Technology Administration

The Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Technology Administration (BTA) program trains students for management position. It is a program to help develop practical knowledge and operation management.

Information Systems

B.S. in Infomration Systems prepares students for all potential career in the IT/IS field. They include subjects like Software Analysis and Design, Database Management, Network, and Software Developer.

Minor in Information Systems

The IS minor is for students that want to expand their horizon in their career. It offers the same benefits for all Information Systems major, but fort students that want to accomplish this as a minor.
